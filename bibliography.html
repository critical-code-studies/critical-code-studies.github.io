Bibliography

Foundations

A short, foundational bibliography for Critical Code Studies as a method, together with key primary and secondary works behind the readings on this site. Each individual reading carries its own fuller bibliography. References are verified against the project's reference library.

The method

Critical Code Studies

  • Marino, M. C. (2006) 'Critical Code Studies', electronic book review, 4 December. The founding manifesto
  • Montfort, N., Baudoin, P., Bell, J., Bogost, I., Douglass, J., Marino, M. C., Mateas, M., Reas, C., Sample, M. and Vawter, N. (2013) 10 PRINT CHR$(205.5+RND(1)); : GOTO 10. Cambridge, MA: MIT Press. Collaborative single-line close reading
  • Marino, M. C. (2020) Critical Code Studies. Cambridge, MA: MIT Press. The book-length statement of the field
  • Marino, M. C. and Douglass, J. (2023) 'Introduction: Situating Critical Code Studies in the Digital Humanities'. Field overview
  • Berry, D. M. (2011) The Philosophy of Software: Code and Mediation in the Digital Age. Basingstoke: Palgrave Macmillan. Code, mediation, and critical theory
ELIZA

The founding reading

  • Weizenbaum, J. (1966) 'ELIZA — A Computer Program for the Study of Natural Language Communication Between Man and Machine', Communications of the ACM, 9(1), pp. 36–45. Primary source
  • Berry, D. M. and Marino, M. C. (2024) 'Reading ELIZA: Critical Code Studies in Action', electronic book review. CCS reading of the recovered source
  • Marino, M. C., Weil, P., Shrager, J., Schwarz, A., Hay, A., Ciston, S., Berry, D. M. and Millican, P. (2026) 'Conversations about conversational code: on the collaborative critical code studies reading of ELIZA'. The collaborative reading
SHRDLU

Symbolic AI and the blocks world

  • Winograd, T. (1971) Procedures as a Representation for Data in a Computer Program for Understanding Natural Language. PhD thesis. Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Issued as MIT AI Laboratory Technical Report AI-TR-235. Primary source
  • Winograd, T. (1972) 'Understanding natural language', Cognitive Psychology, 3(1), pp. 1–191. doi:10.1016/0010-0285(72)90002-3. Primary source
  • Sussman, G. J., Winograd, T. and Charniak, E. (1970) Micro-Planner Reference Manual. MIT AI Laboratory, AI Memo 203. Technical infrastructure
